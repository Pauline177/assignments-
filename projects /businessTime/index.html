United African Women of Hope serves three important purposes:

  • Education: to provide opportunities to women 15 years and older to access basic life skills, guidance to educational resources, social and psychological support to help advance personal and family dynamics as well as social integration and self-sufficiency.
  • Peer Support: Utilize a peer support system as an effective tool to connect a diverse group of refugees. UAWOH’s “Big Sister” network assist and empower women to express themselves freely. They are safe to share personal hardships, family troubles, ethical or moral concerns.
  • Culture Preservation: We recognize and celebrate cultural differences through the arts. From dance classes to music, food and fashion, these women find intrinsic and altruistic values in each other and learn to organize future activities to nurture growth through the arts while preserving their unique cultures.
